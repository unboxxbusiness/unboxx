# Local SEO & AEO Trends for 2026

The search engine marketing environment is changing rapidly. Modern users do not just look at search result links—they ask questions to AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini. To stay visible, local brands must optimize for both traditional maps ranking systems and AI engine indices.

## Core Pillars of 2026 Growth

### 1. Zero-Click Search Optimization
More than half of searches terminate on the map pack or via direct AI answer boxes. Injecting FAQ structure markup and local Business details helps capture these impressions instantly.

### 2. Conversational Citations
AI engines parse reviews to suggest recommendations. Having automated customer loops (e.g. WhatsApp prompts) generating fresh keywords and positive ratings drives recommendations in responses.

### 3. Coordinate Accuracy
Mismatched maps coordinates send prospects to competitors. Unboxx helps synchronize maps pins and listings dynamically.
